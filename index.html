ABOUT US

Welcome to Orbit Casting Agency – your premier partner in connecting talent with opportunities in the entertainment industry. Based in the heart of ethiopia, we specialize in discovering and nurturing diverse talent across film, television, commercials, and theater. What We Do: At Orbit Casting Agency, we are committed to providing a platform for aspiring and established actors, models, and performers. Our team of experienced casting directors and industry professionals works diligently to match the right talent with the right projects, ensuring a seamless casting experience for both clients and performers. Our Services: Casting Calls: We host a variety of casting calls for projects of all sizes, from indie films to major studio productions. We provide comprehensive listings to help talent find the perfect fit. Talent Representation: We offer personalized representation for actors and models, guiding them through the casting process and helping them navigate their careers. Project Collaboration: Our agency collaborates with producers, directors, and production companies to fulfill their casting needs, ensuring that they have access to the best talent available. Workshops and Training: We believe in empowering talent through education. Our workshops cover acting techniques, auditioning skills, and industry insights to help performers hone their craft. Why Choose Us: Expertise: With years of experience in the industry, our team understands the nuances of casting and is dedicated to finding the best talent for every role. Diversity and Inclusion: We prioritize diversity in our casting calls, ensuring representation across all backgrounds, genders, and abilities. Personalized Service: At Orbit Casting Agency, we value the individual journey of each performer. We take the time to understand your goals and provide tailored guidance to help you succeed. Join Us: Whether you’re a seasoned professional or just starting your journey in the entertainment world, Orbit Casting Agency is here to support you every step of the way. Explore our current casting opportunities and let us help you reach for the stars.
